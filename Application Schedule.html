ISEC 2019 Application Schedule
26th Nov 2018 ISEC applications open!
15th April 2018 Last day for all applications!
6th July 2019 ISEC begins!
9th Aug 2019 ISEC ends*
Shortly after completing your application, you will be asked to provide references and to complete a further questionnaire. You will then be interviewed by two members of the ISEC team. This is all to get a sense of your suitability for the program, and to see where you might fit into a team. Interviews are conducted over Skype, and usually take around 45 minutes. We aim to complete the whole application process as soon as we reasonably can, and aim to have decisions out by mid April.
*Early Departure will be only granted to program participants who receive permission from ISEC leadership in writing BEFORE committing to the program.  Please see FAQ for more information.
